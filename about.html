About Us

Rays Pools & Chemical is a proprietorship company headed by Mr. A. S. Venkatesha since 1994. We are specialized in all types of swimming pools and water body works, and we have successfully designed, constructed, and refurbished more than 500 projects across India. Over more than 25 years of experience. We have commissioned projects at different scales for different clients - Residential, Commercial, Institutional, Builders, Developers, Architects, Engineers.

We plan, design and build swimming pools according to the client’s wishes and desires as we pay attention to their lifestyles and requirements. Our aim is to give our clients an opportunity that enables them to choose a catalog of pools suitable for their contexts and budget with a crystal clear experience. We are a highly trained group of talented design professionals including architects, technicians and administration staff for a hassle-free and on-time completion of the projects.

Ecology, Environment, and Conservation is the need of today’s Modern world, our design, and research team closely work with architects and engineers to provide an eco-friendly and sustainable solution in future up-coming projects. In the coming years, we would be glad to share our experience with you and provide your company’s esteemed projects with our world-class innovative solutions.
